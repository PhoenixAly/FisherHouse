# Alaska Fisher House Website

A modern, responsive website for the Alaska Fisher House - providing a home away from home for military families during times of medical need.

## About

The Alaska Fisher House is located on Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson in Anchorage, Alaska, and provides compassionate support and accommodations for service members, veterans, and their families.

## Website Pages

- **Home** - Overview and mission statement
- **Fisher House** - Information about the Fisher House program
- **Immediate Needs** - Donation information and needed items
- **Our Supporters** - List of corporate and individual donors
- **Board of Directors** - Leadership team and representatives
- **Contact Us** - Contact information and contact form
